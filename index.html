I'm a technology and product executive who specialises in turning strategy into execution: building high-performing teams, scalable digital platforms, and resilient business models in complex regulatory environments.

My career spans co-founding Saudi Arabia's largest P2P lending platform, leading AI-driven compliance SaaS across Northern Europe, deploying patented robotics in nuclear facilities, and building IoT platforms for Schneider Electric. Some of these ventures have been genuine success stories. Others, like Easy Comply, didn't reach the finish line. But every one of them sharpened how I think, build, and lead.

Beyond the boardroom, I'm a charity founder working to bring 3 million children into schools, a cook who makes coconut milk from scratch, and a science fiction writer imagining the cities of tomorrow.

Superpower in the Age of AI

My strongest skill is focus. There are more tools, shortcuts, and distractions available today than ever before. Knowing what to ignore is half the job. AI multiplies capability. Focus decides where it goes.

002 / Career

The path so far: wins, lessons, and all

Not every venture ends in triumph, and I believe honesty about that is more valuable than a polished highlight reel. Here's the full journey.

2021 – 2025

Chief Product Officer

Easy Comply AS (prev. Sahal VerifAI)
Oslo, Norway

Led product strategy for an AI-driven compliance SaaS across Northern Europe. Pioneered an AI-native development model, rebuilding engineering from 7 to 2 developers while maintaining full velocity, cutting costs by ~70%. Designed event-driven architecture with NestJS and established product governance that cut time-to-market by 50%. Led 7 direct and 25 indirect reports across multiple geographies. The company ultimately wound down, but the lessons in product-market fit, AI-native building, and resilience under pressure were invaluable.

005 / Giving Back

3 million children into schools

In 2016, I co-founded Alkhidmat Europe with a clear mission: move children from poverty and conflict into classrooms. By 2030, we aim to reach 3 million children across Uganda, Pakistan, Palestine, and beyond.

I served as Board Chairman from 2022 to 2026, during which we grew annual donations from NOK 2 million to 40 million in just three years, driven by operational transparency, digital donation platforms, and relentless field work.

Alkhidmat Europe is now a United Nations Partner Portal member, ranked in the top 20 by WHO for health sector work in Palestine, and operates across 10 countries.

006 / The Kitchen

Everything from scratch

Cooking, for me, is the same discipline as building products: start with the raw materials, understand the process deeply, and never take shortcuts. If it can be made from scratch, it should be.

I don't open a can of coconut milk. I crack baby coconuts and press it myself. I don't buy curry paste. I pound it in a mortar. This isn't about perfectionism. It's about respect for the craft and understanding what you're actually building.

  • 🥥
    Thai Red Curry: The Real Way
    Coconut milk pressed from baby coconuts. Red paste ground by hand. No shortcuts, no cans, no compromises.
  • 🔥
    Drum-Smoked Brisket
    Getting that classic offset smoker bark and smoke ring using a drum smoker. Low, slow, and worth every hour.
  • 🫘
    London's Best Hummus, Recreated
    The recipe from one of London's most famous hummus restaurants, decoded and made at home. Silky, lemony, and absurdly good.

Some of my recipes were published in a book (page 48), though I'll be the first to say they didn't quite capture them as intended. Still, it's a nice reminder that the kitchen is where I think best, right after the whiteboard.

Imagining tomorrow
